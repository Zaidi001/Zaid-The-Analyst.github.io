ABOUT ME

Growing up in a rural setting, I was captivated by our home's first computer. By eleven, I had mastered Microsoft Office and was intrigued by stories of tech pioneers like Bill Gates. This early passion prompted me to delve into programming during my sophomore year, culminating in winning a National Robotics Competition in Pakistan during my junior year.

Although I initially pursued civil engineering in college due to familial expectations, my heart remained with programming. After graduation, I resolutely shifted towards Artificial Intelligence. Overcoming numerous challenges, including multiple visa rejections and the attempting rigorous GRE exam multiple times (328/340 latest score), I was finally accepted into the University at Buffalo's Robotics Master's program.

I have expertise in Python, Linux Bash, SQL and am proficient with machine learning tools such as PyTorch, Pyspark, Keras, OpenCV, TensorFlow, Matplotlib, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Spacy, NLTK, and Hugging Face. My Data Science toolkit includes Tableau, PowerBI, Excel & AWS Services especially S3 , I'm well-versed in deployment and version control with Heroku , Git and GitHub and I'm adept at web application deployment using Flask .

With this blend of passion and expertise, I'm eager to innovate in Machine Learning, Data Science, and Robotics.
